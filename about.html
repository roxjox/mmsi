About Me

Girik Aman

Girik Aman is a well known Punjabi singer when it comes to name some of the respected popular ones in Punjabi music industry who have given their lives to make music unbeatably awesome. Already have 24 hits in punjabi language nd one in kanna language. Girik aman , like several of his counterparts is a first generation musician. Girik aman is a first punjabi singer who introduced bollywood actress sunny leone & sana khan in punjabi industry .. girik aman is also the first punjabi singer , who came bare body with 8 pack abs . He has learnt singing from delhi gharana under the guidance of delhi gharana 'kahalifa' late shri iqbal khan saab . He has given major hits like SAREE WALI GIRL ft. SUNNY LEONE , BLACK TILL ft. Sana khana , TAKE YOUR SANDALS OFF ft. BADSHAH nd many more .... girik aman has also sang a song in a kanada film CARE OF FOOTPATH 2 ,Song name is BULLET NA NA ft. Avika gaur (balika vadhu ) , that movie was nominated for OSCARS too.
